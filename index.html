About Us

Make Your Life Healthy with Neictar

Nectar Asia Pvt Ltd is a leading manufacturer specializing in high-quality feed additives, probiotics, sanitizers, minerals, and gels designed to enhance the health and growth of fish and prawns. Established in 2012 as Nectar Biosciences and rebranded in 2021, we are committed to revolutionizing the aquaculture industry with innovative and sustainable solutions.

  • Innovative Feed Additives for Aquaculture
  • Comprehensive Health Solutions for Fish and Prawns
